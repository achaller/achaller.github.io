Introduction

Poosh Bakery

Welcome to Poosh Bakery! We are a Bakery based out of Calabasas California that hand bakes organically sourced vegan friendly cookies! Our business sells directly to our consumers and is in the food retail industry.We have a large variety of cookie flavors and our products can be questioned in person or online. We also offer local delivery.

Target audience

The target audience for Poosh Bakery are health and wellness millennials ranging in all ages. The people who are using this site are here to browse our products and place orders whether it is an online order or an in store pickup.

The primary desires and needs of our audience is to have a positive user experience when using our website to order our products. Their needs specifically for our products are cookies that are vegan and healthy.
